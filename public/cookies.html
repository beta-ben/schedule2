Cookie Policy

We use only essential cookies required to provide secure access to the application. No analytics, advertising, or cross-site tracking cookies are set.

  • Session cookie (sid): authenticates your admin session. HttpOnly; expires automatically.
  • CSRF token cookie (csrf): protects write actions. HttpOnly; expires automatically.
  • Site session cookie (site_sid): allows read access when required. HttpOnly; expires automatically.
  • Preference cookies (theme, layout): optional and stored in your browser storage.

You can revoke consent by clearing the cookie_consent_v1 item from your browser storage or cookies.
